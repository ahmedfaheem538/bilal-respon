Tummy tuck (Abdominoplasty )

Tummy tuck or abdominoplasty is a surgical procedure that makes your abdomen thinner and firmer. This is achieved by removing the excess skin and fat in your abdominal area. In some cases, liposuction might also be necessary to remove excess fat under the skin. I offer two types of abdominoplasty, depending on the amount of tissue that needs to be removed: formal operation or mini abdominoplasty.
With the help of this procedure, you will be able to enjoy a wider choice of clothes to wear, including tight-fitting clothes and smaller sizes. You will experience increased self-confidence not only towards your body, but other aspects of yourself, and look in the mirror with strong and renewed self-esteem.

Breast Enhancement

For many women, the breasts are an important source of a sense of femininity. This feeling can be affected by the woman’s perception of her own breasts.
One of the greatest advantages of breast enhancement by augmentation, reduction or fat injection, is that they adjust the volume and fullness of your breasts while keeping a natural appearance and consistency. This will boost your confidence and make you feel satisfied about your new image.

Liposuction and Fat Injection

have you ever looked at your body in the mirror and thought about how you would look like with perfect contouring? Many people, regardless of their age and sex, feel dissatisfied about their body contour, due to excess of fatty tissue in a specific region (or regions). Diet is one of the most common ways to approach this problem, but although it can work for overall weight loss, it is not effective when targeting regional excess fat.
If you have an average weight and good skin elasticity but feel concerned about regional excess of fatty tissue, liposuction might be the ideal way for you to achieve the body image and contour that you desire. Plastic surgeons can generally improve your body contour by using macro implants or fat augmentation, but not all of them can deal with subtle details. Some surgeons however, thanks to specialized training, can shape your body with the artistic expertise of a sculptor by using micro fat transplantation.

Scars Management & Camouflage

Scarring is a normal pathological process that happens during the healing of tissue. Its purpose is to protect the internal body by
preventing the discontinuity of the skin.

Unsightly scars can result from multiple factors, including the site and size of the injury, the type of tissue affected, associated factors like infection and sun exposure or medical conditions such as diabetes. Scars can be caused by burns, trauma, surgery, infection or any other agent that results in extensive loss of skin.

Scars might lead to disfigurement or limitation of joint movement, depending on the site and extent of the injury. Additionally, it might cause an unwanted mark that disrupts your aesthetic appearance, which in some cases might lead you to avoid cameras or even social occasions.

Every scar is different and might require treatment by a multidisciplinary team, so specific procedures are planned individually for each case. With a skilled team of plastic surgeons, dermatologists and clinical pathologists will determine the most suitable form of treatment for your case based on medical history, time of injury, additional health conditions such as diabetes and family history.

Some scars will need surgical treatment, but other cases are treatable with modern non-surgical techniques such as fat injection, stem cell technique and PRP (Platelets Rich Plasma) or camouflage by micro pigmentation. Every method is aimed to achieve the safest and most aesthetic result possible.
